KINGDOM COME: DELIVERANCE II - THIRD PERSON CAMERA
Version 1.1.0

INSTALLATION:
1. Extract all files to: <KC:D 2 installation folder>/Bin/Win64MasterMasterSteamPGO/
2. Launch the game; third-person turns on automatically once you reach gameplay.
3. Press F3 (default), or hold LB + RB on a controller, to toggle first/third person.

Hotkeys and settings are in KCD2_TPVCamera.ini. Full details and support:
https://github.com/tkhquang/KCD2Tools
